## Vision

### 10x Check
This page doesn't just describe Safini — it *shows* it. The hero plays a looping animation of a kid completing a Duolingo lesson, coins cascading into their wallet, then unlocking Roblox without an argument. A live counter ticks up: "1,247 families on the waitlist." Trust badges signal: Built with child privacy in mind, No ads, No data sold. The form submits and confetti explodes — followed by a share prompt. Conversion target: 20%+.

### Platonic Ideal
A parent who fought with their kid over a phone tonight lands here at 11pm. In 8 seconds they see the hero. In 30 seconds they feel their exact problem named and solved. In 60 seconds they enter their email. The confirmation page celebrates and invites them to refer another parent. Every section answers a question before it's asked.
