Guidelines

Usage Notes

A few simple rules to keep the brand consistent wherever it appears.

Do

  • Use the provided PNG files without modification.
  • Refer to the product as "Safini" (capital S, no space).
  • Give the mascot adequate clear space — at least its own width on all sides.
  • Use Violet (#8100D1) as the primary accent when the full brand palette isn't available.
  • Contact us before using assets in video, merchandise, or co-branded materials.

Don't

  • Stretch, rotate, recolor, or add effects to the logo or mascot.
  • Spell it "SAFINI", "safini", or "Safini app".
  • Place the logo on a background where contrast is insufficient.
  • Use the mascot in contexts that could be seen as endorsing third-party products.
  • Recreate the logo or mascot from scratch.
